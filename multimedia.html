How to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich!

Step: 1

Ask if yourself if you are hungry.

Step: 2

If you are hungry, head over to your kitchen.

If you are not hungry, you can keep reading if you want but you don't have to.

Step: 3

Gather the needed supplies.

  • 2 pieces of bread for each sandwich
  • Peanut Butter(any kind you like, we don't judge)
  • Any flavor of jelly

Step: 4

Apply the peanut butter to one piece of bread and jelly to the other piece.

Step: 5

Bring the two slices of bread together, making sure the jelly and peanut butter are both on the inside.

Final Step

Enjoy.
