Where to Start?

This page is to make starting Black desert as easy and simple as possible.

Steps

  1. Head to the Black Desert Website. Create an account and download the game client.
  2. Once the game has finished downloading you can choose which class you want to play(there is a class guide on the links page)
  3. Creating your character can seem a little overwhelming with how in-depth the character creator is.
  4. After starting the game will have an easy to follow tutorial and after that follow the main story.
