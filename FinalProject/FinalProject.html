Hello there!

If you are here it is because you are just starting out in Black Desert Online or you are looking into the game to see if it is a game you should try out. Our goal here is to break down the very basics of the game that will not only help your journey in the long run but also help you avoid early obstacles that newer players tend to get stuck on.

What is Black Desert Online?

Black Desert is a Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Game. It has a medieval fantasy setting with an open world to explore. The game has an action style combat system where different key combinations will execute different skills for each class in the game. The graphical quality of the game was quite high even at launch but was upgraded even further in their remastered version. While the game can have extremely high graphic modes it also has a low optimization mode if you either don’t care about visuals or you don’t have a powerful enough computer.

What do you do?

While many of the endgame players are focused on the weekly wars to control the different Nodes and Territories within the world, a new player should focus on leveling up and going through the main storyline. There is no true level cap in the game, there are soft caps in the game where the experience needed is so high that it takes a ridiculously long time to gain even 1% of the needed experience. Gear is also a long-term goal within the game which I will link guides to later. At the end of the day whatever you find enjoyable or exciting about the game is what you should be doing.
